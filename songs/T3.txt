5 Seconds of Summer(5SOS) is an Australian band, One of their song is Teeth.
Teeth was released on August 21, 2019.
Song available on 13 Reasons Why (Season 3) album.
Genres are industrial pop, industrial rock, and grunge.
Songwriters are Ali Tamposi, Ashton Irwin, Luke Hemmings, Andrew Watt, Bernard Sumner, Carl Sturken, Evan Rogers, Peter Hook, Gillian Gilbert, Ryan Tedder, and Stephen Morris.
Record Label of Teeth is Interscope Records.
Song length is 3:24.
The producers of this song are Andrew Watt and Louis Bell.
The song was announced on the band's social media on 16 August 2019, along with a 15-second teaser that Alternative Press called "ominous".
The song was used as the official theme song for WWE Survivor Series 2019 in addition to appearing in ESPN's 2019 Saturday Night Football season.
